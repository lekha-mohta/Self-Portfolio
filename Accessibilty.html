ACCESSIBILITY

Accessibility is a concept whereby tools and technologies are easily accessible by any user (including those with disabilities).

The following are the compliance that have been taken to ensure that this website is easily accessible:

1) Alternative text on Images and Links
For people with physical and visual disabilities, it may be difficult to understand the content and use other alternatives such as speech readers if there are no alternatives to the images and links.

2) The simple color scheme and easy-to-read fonts.
The contrast between background and font colors and proper spacing between various elements makes the website easy to read and understand, even for people with cognitive disabilities. Also, increasing the font size ini general helps make content clearer to people.

3)Easy keyboard navigation and RWD
People without access to computers can also use the website making it more accessible to users. Also, with simpler navigation, people with phsyical and cognitive disabilities can also eaily browse through the website.

This website follows the POUR principle and the recommendation set out by W3C standards to ensure accessibility for all.
